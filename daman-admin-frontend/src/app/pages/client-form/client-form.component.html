App features

These flags are embedded in client.config.json at build time and control the packaged desktop/web app.

Operations
Reports & Statements
Adds the Account Statement nav link — per-client / per-supplier balance history with opening balance, running balance, and date filters.
Adds the Item Ledger nav link — per-product movement history (sales, purchases, stock adjustments) with opening & closing on-hand qty.
Adds the تسوية الجرد المجمّعة page — enter actual on-hand quantities for many products at once and post a single batch adjustment. Differences are written as MONTHLY_RECONCILIATION stock adjustments and flow into the wastage / inventory-gain P&L lines.
Adds the تعديل أسعار قسم بالكامل action on the Products page — increase or decrease every product in a category by a percentage or fixed amount, with separate inputs per pricing currency (base USD vs fixed SYP cards, etc.).
Enables composite products in the Product modal (e.g. Espresso built from coffee beans + sugar). Selling a recipe deducts raw-material stock and rolls up cost dynamically — the finished good does not track its own inventory.
Inventory & Sales Behaviour
Enable for stores selling by weight or bulk (grocery, pharmacy). Disable for stores selling whole units only (mobile phones, electronics) — only whole-number quantities will be accepted at the POS and the "Calculate by Total" tool will only allow adjusting the unit price, not the quantity.
Enable for stores selling products with fixed local-currency prices (MTN / Syriatel top-up cards, voucher products, government-regulated SKUs) when the system base currency is different (e.g. USD-based POS in Syria). A new "Pricing Currency" dropdown appears on the Product modal so each SKU can be locked to its native currency; the POS converts to base on the fly using the invoice's currency rate so totals, profit, and the cash drawer stay coherent. Leave off for stores that always price in the base currency.
